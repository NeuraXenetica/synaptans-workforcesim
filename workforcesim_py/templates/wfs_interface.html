Synaptans WorkforceSim™

Synaptans WorkforceSim is a free open-source platform for simulating the complex dynamics of a factory workforce, including workers’ daily job performance, personal interactions with their peers and supervisors, and attrition. The results of a given run of the simulation are analyzed through a series of visualizations and outputted as a CSV file containing all of the behaviors that workers and managers displayed throughout the simulated period. The results’ format and contents have been designed for use in training machine-learning models to identify hidden trends and correlations in workers’ behavior and to facilitate objective assessments of the accuracy of differing approaches to predictive workplace analytics.
